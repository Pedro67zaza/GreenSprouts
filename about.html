About GreenSprouts

Empowering plant lovers with innovative technology and a passion for sustainable growth

Our Story

GreenSprouts began with a simple observation: plant care shouldn't be complicated. Founded in 2025, we saw how traditional gardening methods could be enhanced with modern technology to make plant care accessible to everyone.

Our journey started when the founder, Afizudin Firdaus, struggled to keep plants alive. The challenges involved lack of knowledge, difficulty in tracking, remembering, and applying that knowledge consistently. That's when the idea for GreenSprouts was born.

Today, GreenSprouts plan to serve thousands of plant enthusiasts worldwide, from beginners taking their first steps into gardening to experienced horticulturists managing extensive collections.

Our Mission

To democratize plant care by providing intelligent, accessible tools that help everyone, from beginners to experts, grow thriving, healthy plants while fostering a global community of plant lovers who support and inspire each other.

Our Vision

A world where every person has the knowledge, confidence, and tools to cultivate green spaces, contributing to a healthier planet and more connected communities through the shared joy of growing plants.
