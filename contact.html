Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with GreenSprouts?
Getting started is easy! Simply click the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page, create your free account, and you'll have immediate access to all our features.
Is GreenSprouts free to use?
Yes! Our free services include access to our digital logbook, basic plant repository, community feed, AI chatbot assistance, detailed growth statistics, and vast plant entries.
What kind of plants does GreenSprouts support?
GreenSprouts pulls its repository from the OpenPlantBook API. Thus, our database includes what can be found on OpenPlantBook, such as houseplants, outdoor garden plants, vegetables, herbs, succulents, and more.
